Pro Tip: The 3-2-1 Review Method

Review your Cornell notes 3 times: immediately after class, 2 days later, and 1 week later. This spaced repetition locks information into long-term memory.

3-Step Cornell Routine

  3. Recite: Use cues to test yourself before exams

Everything you need to know about Cornell note-taking

How is Cornell different from regular notes?

Cornell notes use a structured three-section format that promotes active learning and review, unlike traditional linear note-taking.

Can I use this method digitally?

Absolutely! Our templates work great in Notion, OneNote, Google Docs, or any digital note-taking app.

How long does it take to see results?

Most students notice improved focus immediately, with significant retention improvements within 2-3 weeks of consistent use.

Is this suitable for all subjects?

Yes! Cornell notes work exceptionally well for lectures, reading comprehension, research, and even creative brainstorming.
